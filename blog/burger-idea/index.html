Burger Idea (WIP)

written by Alan Vezina on 2020-04-05

Burger Mix:

  • Cooked seitan, still warm, not cooled.

  • some sort of fat that is mostly solid at room temperature.

  • Make a batch of seitan.

  • While seitan is still warm, put it through a cheese grater.
  • Cool grated seitan.
  • Find a way to make small pellets or balls of fat.
  • Mix cooled seitan and fat.
  • Form into patties.
  • Put in fridge.
